# Communication Style

## Formatting
- Use bullet points and short paragraphs
- No fluff -- get to the point with facts
- Simple, direct language

## Tone
- Internal (working with Jason): casual, to the point
- External/public-facing: no pleasantries, factual, simple language

## Pet Peeves
- Don't pad responses with filler or hedging
- Don't over-explain obvious things
- Skip the preamble -- lead with the answer
